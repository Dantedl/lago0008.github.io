Retro poster

This poster defined an era of personal growth. This poster was my last assignment for my Digital graphics class. This was one of the most challenging assignments I ever had. It was challenging in the sense of difficulty, but in the sense of making it look nice. Anyone can photoshop a picture. However, editing and making sure every tiny detail goes according to your sense of design is difficult. It made me think of who I was, what were my inspiration. etc.

Dogetopus

The adorable dogetopus was my biggest creation. The art of combining two different animals is a huge challenge. Space cat

This alien cat was my midterm for digital graphics. For this assignment I had to design 3 different logos for 3 different platforms. I had to develop an icon for an Iphone, an icon for a tablet and an icon for a computer. The result of hours hours of looking for inspiration, I came across a picture of an alien cat. This alien cat was the result of adding my touch into that alien cat.
